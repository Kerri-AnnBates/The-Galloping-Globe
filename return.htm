Return Policies

  • Most hotel rooms are fully refundable with no penalty fees if cancelled by the hotel's cancellation deadline. If you booked a non-refundable room, you will not receive a refund if you change or cancel the booking.
  • We process your refund on the day you cancel your booking.
  • We apply refunds to your original payment card.
  • Some fares are non-refundable. Review your itinerary for fare rules, restrictions, and ticket change fees
  • Most credit card companies post your refund to your account within 2 weeks, and it will appear on your statement within 2 billing cycles.
